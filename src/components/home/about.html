How it works

  • Creators:

    Register your business

    Once your application is approved, we will work with you to create realistic milestone plan (written into a smart contract), which will be shown to potential backers

  • Creators:

    Begin your crowdfunding round

    During this period, backers can invest in your idea

  • Backers:

    Invest in creators you like!

    When you back a creator, you will be issued CRWN tokens! Your investment will be held in an escrow account, which will release funds to the creators as they complete their milestones!

  • Creators:

    Complete Milestones

    As you complete your milestones, your funding will be released to you! When your business brings in revenue, you pay your backers a percentage of your profits in royalties according to have many CRWN tokens they were issued.

    Wins!
